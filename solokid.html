Guitar Skills

I specialize in soulful solos with a bluesy feel, often using F# minor scale, delay and echo effects, and expressive techniques like bends, slides, and hammer-ons. My guitar of choice is the PRS CE Custom 24.

Guitars I Recommend

Electric Guitars

PRS CE Custom 24

This is my favorite electric guitar, known for its sharp, versatile tone and amazing sustain.

Gibson Les Paul Standard

Classic rock tones with a rich, warm sound. A must-have for any serious guitarist.

Fender Stratocaster

The iconic Strat is perfect for a wide range of styles, from rock to blues.

Ibanez RG550

Fast neck and powerful pickups, perfect for shredders and metal players.

Gretsch G2622 Streamliner

Great for clean, jazzy tones as well as rockabilly and alternative styles.

Acoustic Guitars

Martin D-28

A true legend in acoustic guitars with a rich, balanced tone.

Gibson J-45

The 'workhorse' of acoustic guitars, known for its warm, full sound.

Taylor 314ce

A fantastic mid-range guitar with great clarity and a comfortable playability.

Yamaha FG800

Great value acoustic guitar with a clear, resonant sound.

Fender CD-60S

Affordable and perfect for beginners, yet offers great sound quality.

Classical Guitars

Yamaha C40

An excellent classical guitar for beginners, offering great sound and value.

Cordoba C5

Well-crafted guitar with a full, warm sound. Ideal for classical guitar players.

Takamine GC1

A solid classical guitar with a bright, balanced tone, perfect for solo performances.

Alhambra 3C

A great choice for intermediate classical guitarists looking for a well-rounded sound.

La Patrie Etude

A beautifully crafted classical guitar that offers a warm, vibrant tone.
